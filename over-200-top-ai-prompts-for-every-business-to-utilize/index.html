3 quick tips to write the best AI prompts for your marketing

Remember to always review and refine the output from your AI tools, no matter how good the initial prompt is. This ensures a human touch in the final product. AI, after all, lacks the contextual understanding of a human.

However, following some key guidelines for your AI prompts can streamline the editing process. For more AI tips, tricks, and ideas, check out our free, comprehensive guide to AI in marketing!

1. Start with understanding your AI tool options

Every AI tool is unique in its construction and data access, leading to variations in responses even with identical prompts. Before committing to a specific task, explore the capabilities of different AI tools like ChatGPT, Jasper, and others.

You’ll likely discover that a combination of AI tools works best for your business needs. This is a common practice, as data from AI statistics reveals that small businesses often leverage multiple AI tools.

2. Align AI with your marketing strategy

Your current marketing objectives play a crucial role in selecting appropriate AI tools and crafting relevant prompts. Take a moment to pinpoint areas in your marketing where AI integration makes the most sense.

For instance, if brand awareness is your goal, focus on refining social media AI prompts to rapidly expand your online presence.

3. What to include in an AI prompt

A foolproof AI prompt writing tip: be incredibly specific.

Let’s say I want AI to draft a section for this blog post. My initial prompt would differ significantly from the final, refined version. I might start with “Write a short 3-sentence intro to a blog post about AI prompts.”

However, it would require several iterations to arrive at a publishable intro. The final AI prompt might look like this:

“Write a concise, simple, and engaging 3-sentence introduction for a blog post titled ‘AI Prompt Examples and Ideas for Your Marketing’. The tone should be educational yet approachable and friendly, targeting small businesses. Acknowledge that AI prompts can be challenging, but highlight that this blog post offers solutions. For voice, tone, and style, refer to these blog posts:”

This detailed prompt, though lengthy, emphasizes that editing and refinement are essential for bringing AI content to life. A well-crafted prompt minimizes editing effort. Here are some elements to always include in your AI prompts:

  • Desired response length: Prevent rambling by specifying the desired length of the AI’s response (one sentence, one paragraph, etc.).

  • Business industry: Provide context by specifying your industry.

  • Target audience: Enable AI to tailor its tone to resonate with your target market.

  • Goal of the prompt: Clarify your objective, allowing the AI tool to incorporate a call to action if necessary.

  • Brand style guide: Share your brand kit (via a link or a brief outline within the prompt) so AI understands your expected voice and style.

  • Include links or files of exemplary content for the AI to analyze. This helps the tool mimic a specific competitor or source of inspiration.

AI prompts for keyword optimization

Remember that AI lacks access to the comprehensive data available to professional keyword research tools. Therefore, do not rely solely on AI for keyword research or finalizing your list of keywords. However, you can use AI tools to review your keyword lists and perform similar checks.
