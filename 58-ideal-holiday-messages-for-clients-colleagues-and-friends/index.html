Holiday Greetings for Anyone

These general holiday greetings work for any audience. They’re warm and classic, but if you want to add even more originality, explore our guide to cliche-free holiday copywriting.

  • Wishing you and your loved ones a joyous holiday season! May this time be filled with blessings and lead to a prosperous new year.
  • Happy holidays! We hope this season brings you an abundance of joy and energy for a happy and healthy start to the new year.
  • Season’s greetings! May this time of year bring you peace, joy, and love in abundance.
  • Happy holidays! Wishing you love, light, and laughter, creating wonderful memories and a bright, healthy 2023.
  • Happy holidays to you and your family! May your present be merry, your past cherished, and your future prosperous.
  • Warm greetings during this festive season! May you experience the joy of laughter, the comfort of love, and the warmth of gratitude now and always.
  • We wish you a joyous holiday season! May you enjoy this special time with loved ones and have a delightful winter.

Holiday Greetings for Business

Need the right words for a holiday email, postcard, Christmas card, or social media post? Look no further than these business-appropriate greetings:

  • No promotions here, just warm wishes for joy, peace, and happiness from our team to yours!
  • To our valued colleagues, customers, partners, and friends: Merry Christmas, Happy Hanukkah, and Happy Kwanzaa to all!
  • Merry Christmas and happy holidays from everyone at [business name]! You brighten our days, and we’re eager to continue serving you in the new year.
  • Happy holidays from the team at [business name]. We appreciate your business and look forward to being your trusted [service] provider in the year ahead!
  • As we reflect on the past year, we’re incredibly grateful for customers and partners like you who make our success possible. Thank you for everything you do. Wishing you all the best this holiday season and beyond.
  • As the year comes to a close, we extend our heartfelt gratitude for your loyalty. We’re excited for a new year of collaboration and success. Have a wonderful holiday season, filled with joy!
  • In deep appreciation for your continued support, we extend our warmest holiday wishes to you and your loved ones. Enjoy this special time, and we look forward to connecting in the new year!
  • Dear [name]: Your loyalty and support have been instrumental to our success this year, and we’re incredibly grateful! Thank you. Wishing you a joyous holiday season and a prosperous new year!
  • From everyone at [name of business], Happy Holidays! We appreciate your ongoing support. Take this time to relax, recharge, and celebrate. We’ll see you next year!
  • From all of us at [business name]: Thank you for a fantastic year! Let’s take a break to relax and celebrate, then come back reenergized for another successful year. Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.

  • Dear [client name]. You bring goodwill and cheer to our office year-round, not just during the holidays. We appreciate your positive spirit and can’t wait to see you in the new year. May your holiday season be merry and bright, and your new year filled with happiness!

  • Season’s greetings from the cheerful team at [company name]. You keep us inspired all year long, and we look forward to supporting your [industry] needs in the coming year! Thank you for being you. Happy holidays and a Happy New Year!
  • Happy holidays to all of our amazing clients! Working with you is a true privilege. We wish you a season of joy and another year of shared success!
  • Working with clients like you makes our jobs a joy! From the team at [business name], we extend our sincere gratitude and warmest wishes for a happy holiday season and a healthy new year.
