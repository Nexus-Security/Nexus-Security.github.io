76 Creative Valentine’s Day Instagram Captions (Say Goodbye to Generic Lists!)

Jul 12, 2022
7 minute read

Okay, so you want Valentine’s Day Instagram captions that strike the right balance between cheesy and boring.

Remember those little Valentine’s cards we used to give out in elementary school? I’ve put together a list of 76 Valentine’s Day Instagram captions and templates that are unique and adaptable, categorized for different purposes:

  • Promoting your business to customers
  • Creative and funny captions
  • Statistics and quotes about Valentine’s Day

Valentine’s Day Instagram Captions for Promotions

  • “Forget the sugar rush, show your love with a gift that keeps on giving! Our [products] are the perfect way to say ‘I love you’ without the toothache.”
  • “This Valentine’s Day, choose a gift that lasts longer than a dozen roses…choose [your product]! [Unless you’re a fan of dried flowers, of course.]”
  • “Mother Teresa said we can only achieve small things with great love. We disagree! Check out this [BIG promotion/event/deal].”
  • “Our business thrives on your love! Thanks for spreading the word and keeping our hearts full. [Image of referral program info]”
  • “Looking for true love this Valentine’s Day? Look no further! [insert dreamy picture of your product]”
  • “This Valentine’s Day, fall head over heels for [your value proposition/product/service/feature or benefit of your product].”
  • “Sometimes the smallest gestures mean the most…[picture of your small products].”
  • “Who says bouquets are only for flowers? [share photo of your product in a bouquet]”

A bouquet that lasts as long as your love?

  • “You know it’s real when [we offer you this deal/take this action relevant to your audience or industry].”
  • “How can we steal your heart? Share your thoughts with us! We can handle a little tough love too.” (This one’s great for gathering reviews!)
  • “Cupid must have struck us because we’re giving away [big offer].”
  • “We miss you! Let’s rekindle the flame this Valentine’s Day. What do you say? [re-engagement promotion offer].”
  • “This Valentine’s Day, treat yourself to double the love with double the rewards points!”
  • “You have your Valentine, and I have mine: [Your product].”

Valentine’s Day Instagram Captions for Customer Appreciation

  • “We love you more than [something nerdy or cherished in your niche], and that’s saying something!”
  • “We love you times [something in large quantity in your niche]—that’s a whole lot of love!”
  • “The world needs more customers like you! Can we make a copy?”
  • “You had us at hello // You had us at hell no [no fees/big prices/pain points].”
  • “Our business wouldn’t be the same without amazing customers like you. Thank you!”
  • “Need a Valentine’s date? We’ve got you covered.” [Date of sale or event with details.]
  • “You’re the bee’s knees…the cat’s pajamas…the [ants pants/something comically rhymey that makes sense to your customers.]”
