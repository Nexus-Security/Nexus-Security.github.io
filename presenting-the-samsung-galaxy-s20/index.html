Presenting the Samsung Galaxy S20

Apr 05, 2024
7 minute read

Samsung Electronics has launched the Samsung Galaxy S20, its newest flagship device series, featuring a redesigned camera system.

‚ÄúAs we begin this decade, 5G will revolutionize how we communicate and experience the world,‚Äù said Dr. TM Roh, President and Head of Mobile Communications Business, Samsung Electronics.

‚ÄúWith all three Galaxy S20 variants offering 5G connectivity, Samsung provides a next-generation device that will change people‚Äôs lives. Its incredible, AI-powered camera lets you capture moments instantly and connect more smoothly with loved ones.‚Äù

The entire Galaxy S20 lineup‚Äîthe Galaxy S20, Galaxy S20+, and Galaxy S20 Ultra‚Äîincludes the newest 5G technology. All three devices provide both non-standalone and standalone 5G capabilities. The Galaxy S20+ and Galaxy S20 Ultra are the first to utilize both sub-6 and mmWave*, while the Galaxy S20 supports sub-6*.

Samsung recognizes the camera as the most crucial aspect for consumers choosing a new smartphone. The Galaxy S20’s innovative camera system uses artificial intelligence (AI) and boasts its most substantial image sensor yet.

Clarity

The Galaxy S20 series’ larger image sensor significantly increases camera resolution for more detailed pictures. The S20 and S20+ feature a 64 MP camera, while the S20 Ultra boasts a 108 MP camera.

These larger sensors also capture more light, improving image quality even in low-light conditions. The S20 Ultra goes further, allowing dynamic switching between a high-resolution 108 MP mode and a 12 MP mode using nona-binning technology, which combines nine pixels into one at the sensor level.

Zoom

The Galaxy S20‚Äôs Space Zoom technology combines Hybrid Optic Zoom and AI-powered Super Resolution Zoom. Users can access up to 30X zoom on the Galaxy S20 and S20+ or utilize the S20 Ultra’s revolutionary folded lenses with AI-powered, multi-image processing to minimize quality loss at high zoom levels. This allows for Super Resolution Zoom up to 100X with unprecedented clarity.

Single Take, Multiple Possibilities

The Galaxy S20 leverages its AI camera technologies to capture various photos and videos simultaneously, including live focus, cropped, ultra-wide, and more, ensuring the perfect shot every time.

Pro-Grade Filming

The Galaxy S20 offers 8K video recording, streamable to a Samsung QLED 8K for an unmatched viewing experience1. High-resolution photos can be created from 8K video stills. Super Steady technology, with its anti-rolling stabilization and AI motion analysis, makes shaky footage look like it was shot with an action camera.

Samsung emphasizes the Galaxy S20’s design for today’s mobile phone usage.

Music

Integrating Spotify with Samsung’s Bixby Routines allows users to enjoy personalized soundtracks based on their daily activities. With Music Share, a paired Bluetooth connection extends to car stereos or speakers through the device2.

Video Chat

The Galaxy S20, with its 5G capabilities, enhances video chatting through deep integration with Google Duo. Experience new Duo features first on the Galaxy S20. Users can initiate a Duo video call directly from the dialer and enjoy FHD quality for the first time. Google Duo’s cross-operating system compatibility allows video chats with anyone. The wide lens fits everyone in the frame for group calls with up to eight friends.

YouTube

Samsung’s partnership with YouTube allows direct uploading of 8K videos to the platform, utilizing integrated 5G capabilities for ultra-fast upload speeds3.

Netflix

Netflix and Samsung are collaborating to equip renowned directors with the Galaxy S20 to film bonus content for popular Netflix Originals, taking advantage of the phone’s professional-grade camera. Improved integration with Galaxy devices enhances the Netflix experience, simplifying content discovery and access through Samsung Daily, Bixby, and _Finder_4.

Gaming

Gaming is smoother than ever with a 120 Hz display. In Q2 2020, Samsung partner Microsoft will launch its popular Forza Street game exclusively in the Galaxy Store, marking its mobile debut. The Galaxy S20 delivers a powerful gaming experience with its fast processor, 12 GB of RAM5, audio tuned by AKG, and a game booster for optimized peak performance settings.

Source: Singtel eDM. Singtel offers free Galaxy Buds+ with preorders of the Galaxy S20 and S20 Ultra 5G. Singtel exclusives include free months of Spotify Premium and Viu Premium, along with discounts on its Mobile Swop service.

Samsung touts the Galaxy S20 as its most secure device yet. It is protected by Knox, a mobile security platform providing protection from the chip level to the software level. A new, secure processor safeguards against hardware-based attacks.

The Galaxy S20 series features a high-capacity, intelligent battery, a 25 W fast charger, and 45 W Super Fast charging support for the S20 Ultra6. All models have substantial standard storage (128 GB for the S20; 128 GB, 256 GB, and 512 GB options for the Galaxy S20+ and S20 Ultra)7.

The Galaxy S20 utilizes One UI 2, Samsung‚Äôs cleanest, simplest, and most intuitive interface. Control smart homes with SmartThings, manage health and wellness goals with Samsung Health, and experience mobile payments through Samsung Pay8, all from the Galaxy S20. Samsung Galaxy Buds+ are designed to complement the Galaxy S20 series. Featuring AKG sound, Buds+ boast two-way speakers, three microphones for enhanced audio and call quality, and up to 11 hours of battery life, extendable to 22 hours with the charging case9. The Galaxy Buds+ app is now compatible with iOS10.
