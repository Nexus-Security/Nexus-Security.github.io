Joey Yap provides predictions for the 12 animal signs in the year 2018

Oct 16, 2022
17 minute read

Source: Joey Yap Private Limited.
Infographic for the 12 animal signs in 2018.

Chinese culture places significance on the lunar year, believing it brings new fortunes. Each of the 12 animal signs in the Chinese zodiac is thought to face unique opportunities and obstacles.

Within the Chinese zodiac, each year aligns with one of 12 animals and one of five elements. Although the year of the fire rooster (January 28, 2017, to February 15, 2018) is still ongoing, a shift is anticipated on February 16, 2018, as the year of the earth dog commences.

Below are predictions for the year of the dog, as shared by Dato’ Joey Yap, a renowned speaker, author, and the Chief Consultant at Joey Yap Private Limited, a consultancy specializing in fengshui and Chinese astrology:

Dog - individuals born in 1934, 1946, 1958, 1970, 1982, 1994, 2006, 2018, and 2030*

Overall Forecast

  • 2018 presents a blend of experiences for Dogs, encompassing both personal and professional hurdles despite achievements.

  • Challenges in one area of life might impact others. For instance, a positive work environment could inadvertently lead to personal life issues.

  • Successfully navigating these challenges hinges on your ability to apply the resilience gained from your career to personal matters.

Wealth

  • Financial worries might persist.

  • The weaker influence of the Indirect Luck Star (偏财) suggests that fortune might not be on your side.

  • Financial prudence is crucial. Prioritize saving, refrain from gambling, and carefully assess new contracts.

Career

  • Anticipate work-related stress and demanding superiors, potentially hindering productivity.

  • Perseverance and consistency are vital in managing this demanding year.

  • Changing jobs is ill-advised. Instead, maximize your current situation.

Relationships (Romance)

  • Romantic prospects seem unfavorable, posing challenges for couples.

  • Married couples should prioritize patience as even trivial disagreements can strain the relationship.

  • Single individuals might face a period of social isolation, suggesting that focusing on other aspects of life may be more fruitful.

Health

  • Road safety demands utmost attention. Lack of vigilance, whether walking or driving, could result in serious injuries. 

  • Exercise caution when handling metal objects due to potential rust or harmful substances.

  • Digestive issues may arise, making a balanced diet and regular exercise essential.

Pig - individuals born in 1935, 1947, 1959, 1971, 1983, 1995, 2007, 2019, and 2031

Overall Forecast

  • The year 2018 offers a prime opportunity for Pigs to showcase their skills, potentially leading to desired promotions and business growth.

  • Increased popularity is also on the horizon, making it easier to form friendships and present successful business propositions that attract promising investments.

  • However, the demands of a vibrant social life can be tiring. Allocating personal time for relaxation and stress relief is crucial to prevent burnout.

Wealth

  • Financial obstacles might surface early in the year, potentially manifesting as stagnant income or savings. A proactive and optimistic approach can swiftly address these issues.

  • Following this, a surge in wealth through a promotion or bonus is likely. However, recovering loaned money might prove challenging.

Career

  • Colleagues will provide valuable assistance, aiding in overcoming minor workplace hurdles. These alliances will be instrumental in navigating challenges to productivity.

  • This support will allow you to demonstrate your abilities to appreciative superiors.

Relationships (Romance)

  • Each year, specific stars with unique influences are linked to different zodiac signs. For Pigs, the presence of the Sky Happiness Star (天喜) suggests that love will find its way to you, contrasting with last year’s predictions.

  • 2018 is poised to be filled with joyful events. Long-term couples should consider marriage before the year concludes.

  • Single individuals have a high probability of finding love and should be receptive to potential partners within their social sphere.

Health

  • Health prospects appear unfavorable this year.

  • Unwittingly participating in ill-fated events could negatively impact both physical and mental well-being.

  • The protective influence of the Sky Happiness Star can shield you from harm if you maintain a consistent exercise routine and avoid risky physical activities.

Rat - individuals born in 1924, 1936, 1948, 1960, 1972, 1984, 1996, 2008, 2020 and 2032

Overall Forecast

  • Empowered by the Eight Seats Star (八座), 2018 encourages Rats to prioritize personal growth.

  • Expect increased fortune and enhanced learning abilities. This is an opportune time to acquire new skills, refine existing ones, or pursue further education to enhance your capabilities.

  • This boost will be particularly advantageous for those in academia, research, or pursuing studies.

Wealth

  • Be prepared for potential financial setbacks due to unwise investments or delayed repayments. Mitigate this by considering real estate purchases early in the year and avoiding risky investment schemes.

  • Overall, financial matters appear stable. 

Career

  • A turbulent career year lies ahead for Rats. Focus on professional development rather than engaging in workplace politics.

  • Petty conflicts will eventually subside, allowing you to reap the rewards of your efforts, such as promotions or salary increases.

  • Embrace support from helpful individuals to establish career stability in 2018.

Relationships (Romance)

  • This year favors new romantic relationships. Single Rats should be attentive within their social circles to encounter a potential partner.

  • Couples might face health challenges, potentially even hospitalization.

  • Encouraging your partner to engage in physical activity and improving your joint finances to afford healthier food choices can be beneficial.

Health

  • The presence of the Blood Knife Star (血刃) necessitates heightened attention to your well-being.

  • Avoid risky physical activities like hiking to minimize the risk of serious injuries. Fortunately, the Nobleman (天乙貴人) Star’s influence suggests that help will be available if health issues arise.

Ox - individuals born in 1913, 1925, 1937, 1949, 1961, 1973, 1985, 1997, 2009, 2021 and 2033

Overall Forecast

  • This year presents a favorable time for Oxen to enhance their financial standing. Seize opportunities as they arise.

  • Industries dominated by women are likely to thrive, and you’ll receive timely support from women in your life. A female senior manager might provide valuable advice or even advocate for your promotion. Embrace their generosity.

Wealth

  • The presence of the Moon Star (太阴) in your Wealth Palace indicates excellent potential for wealth accumulation in 2018. According to fengshui principles, an individual’s fortune or physical spaces can be divided into nine “palaces,” each governing a specific life aspect such as career, family, and wealth. 

  • An unexpected pay raise through bonuses or a promotion is possible.

  • While your financial outlook is positive, exercise caution by investing in stable assets like real estate and avoiding risky financial ventures.

Career

Relationships (Romance)

  • The absence of the Peach Blossom Star (桃花) suggests challenges for single Oxen seeking relationships. However, socializing remains important as you might still encounter a potential partner.

  • Stable couples should proceed with wedding plans.

Health

  • The presence of an unfavorable star in your palace signifies potential health challenges.

  • Medical expenses might become commonplace if health is neglected.

Tiger - individuals born in 1914, 1926, 1938, 1950, 1962, 1974, 1986, 1998, 2010, 2022 and 2034

Career

  • Disputes arising from miscommunication with superiors or colleagues are likely. Be mindful of your interactions.

  • Stay vigilant for colleagues with malicious intentions who might try to undermine you.

  • The Three Stages Star (三台) offers some protection from workplace hostility. Focus on enhancing your communication skills to minimize potential conflicts.

Relationships (Romance)

  • The Three Stages Star symbolizes unity. Committed unmarried couples should seriously consider marriage to strengthen their bond.

  • Single Tigers seeking love should remain optimistic despite the absence of strong Peach Blossom luck as the Three Stages Star’s influence remains favorable.

Health

  • Unfavorable stars in your palace indicate potential health risks, demanding extra vigilance.

  • Consider participating in team sports and seek immediate medical attention if you fall ill.

Rabbit - individuals born in 1939, 1951, 1963, 1975, 1987, 1999, 2011, 2023 and 2035

Career

Relationships (Romance)

Health

Wealth

Career

  • Maintain motivation, resilience, and focus on navigating these challenges.

  • While you might feel stagnant, this is not an ideal year for Dragons to change jobs. Instead, adopt a positive mindset, prioritizing humility and patience.

Relationships (Romance)

  • With weak Peach Blossom luck, unmarried couples should focus on nurturing their existing relationship.

  • Married couples need to be extra considerate as even minor misunderstandings could escalate into arguments.

  • Single Dragons might experience a period of loneliness. Focus on self-improvement and other fulfilling aspects of life rather than solely pursuing romantic prospects.

Health
