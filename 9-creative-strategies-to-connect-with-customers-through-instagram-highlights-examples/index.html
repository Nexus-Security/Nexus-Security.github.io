What are Instagram Highlights?

Instagram Highlights, or Instagram Story Highlights, are curated selections of images, videos, and content previously shared on your Instagram Story. These collections, often called “albums,” are typically organized by themes.

Unlike ephemeral Instagram Stories, Highlights remain on your profile indefinitely, allowing you to adjust content at any time. They’re positioned just below your bio and above your regular feed.

9 ways to use Instagram Highlights to grow your business

Simply lumping all your Instagram Stories into a single Highlight isn’t appealing and won’t encourage interaction. This feature shines with multiple highlights showcasing the different kinds of Stories you create. Here are nine ideas to engage your followers and customers:

1. Showcase your products

Highlighting your products or services is a fantastic way to promote your business and engage customers.

Instagram reports that 75% of users take an action, such as visiting websites or making purchases, after seeing ads on their platform. Regardless of past interactions with your business, active Instagram users browsing your profile might be more enticed by a dedicated “Products” highlight than scrolling through your entire feed.

This highlight becomes a quick and easy way for them to find information, and you can direct them to your website, newsletter signup, or ideally, to make a purchase directly through Instagram. Tagging products in your highlights using Instagram Stories stickers (interactive elements for engagement) allows for creative and less sales-y showcasing.

Let’s see how Letterfolk, a home decor company selling unique paper goods and gifts, showcases their products.

Letterfolk organizes their Story Highlights to display various products like tile mats, calendars, ABC prints, and more. This helps users find specific information, and it’s a great approach for separate highlights focused on seasonal collections, new releases, promotions, and more. Depending on the scale of your offerings, you might consolidate all product-related Stories into one album.

Using the ‚ÄúSee More‚Äù link in these Stories is a great way to incorporate a CTA, engaging followers and leading them to your website for more information.

Instagram Highlights frequently asked questions

Let’s address some common queries about Instagram Story Highlights.

1. Can someone see if I view their Instagram Story Highlight?

Yes, but only for the first 48 hours after the Story was initially posted.

2. How do Instagram Story Highlights work?

To create one, go to your profile, tap ‚ÄúStory highlights," and choose a Story to add. To view a Highlight, visit an account’s profile and click on one of the Story Highlight icons.

3. What is the difference between a Story and a Story Highlight on Instagram?

A Story appears at the top of an Instagram user‚Äôs feed and lasts for 24 hours. A Story Highlight, however, is a Story added to a profile and remains visible until manually removed by the account owner.

4. Can I add an Instagram Highlight without sharing it as a Story first?

There’s a workaround. Make your account private, block all users from seeing your Stories in the settings, post your Story, and add it to your Highlights. After 24 hours, once the Story disappears, revert your account to its previous privacy settings.
