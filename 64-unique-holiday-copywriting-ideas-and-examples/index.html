1. Utilize Tweets for relatable humor

What makes Twitter a valuable resource for fresh copywriting is that effective Tweets are both relatable and remarkably concise. And shouldn’t all impactful copy strive for these qualities? Some Tweets will spark ideas for your own engaging ad copy, while others can be directly integrated into your content for context (such as in a gift guide). To discover relevant holiday Tweets:

  • Conduct a Google search using phrases like funniest Tweets about the holidays or relatable Tweets about winter.
